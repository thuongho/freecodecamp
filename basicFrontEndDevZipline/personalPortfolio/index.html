Thuong "tune" Ho

The name is Thuong, but pronounced like "Tune". Perhaps hearing my name over and over has sparked a passion for music in me. Along with being an Above & Beyond groupie, I am also a dysfunctional guitarist. In the very least, if I get stranded on an island with a guitar, I can still jam on some tunes. :)

As the days go by, I find myself enjoying the outdoors more and more. Whenever I can find the time, I try to catch the sun retiring for the day. Something about the sun reeling in its last rays of light, like a sorcerer casting a magical spell over the land and robbing it of light, is quite heartwarming to watch.

Why do I code? A question I quite feverishly ask myself while dealing with the challenges that comes with this madness called coding. Night in and night out, the unsolved mysteries haunt me, slowly eating away at my sanity. Taunting me as I stare it in the eye for it knows my victory is quite faint.
